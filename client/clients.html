About Us

SET Recruitment was formed out of the success of its sister company MPS Healthcare, having been an integral staffing provider to the NHS and embedded in the recruitment sector since 2000. The business operates throughout Wales with a staffing structure designed to expertly match education workers’ skill sets and experience to local schools, for covering short- and long-term supply assignments.

We are recognised for our stringent and robust compliance vetting procedures, customer experience and specialist approach to school recruitment. Our commitment to the education worker is at the forefront of everything we do as a business. Our welcome meeting will ensure we tailor the needs of the individual to best match the needs of each school’s staffing request, demonstrating excellence and outstanding service levels to all our stakeholders.
